    About Jerry Pizza

    Who We Are

    Jerry Pizza is Sahiwal's #1 family restaurant, located at the heart of Pakpattan Chowk. Since our opening in 2023, we've been committed to serving the finest hand-tossed pizzas with cheese-stuffed golden edges that keep customers coming back.

    What started as a passion for quality food has grown into a beloved local restaurant trusted by families, students, and pizza lovers across Sahiwal.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is simple: deliver the best pizza experience with every order. We believe in using fresh, premium ingredients and hand-crafting every pizza with care.

    From our signature cheese-stuffed crust to our zinger burgers and crispy wings, every item is made fresh to order. We're not just selling food—we're creating memories and satisfying cravings.
